### Importance of practise
1. Practise is the key to success
2. A well prepared speech is already nine-tenths delivered.


### Preparing material
1. A speech/talk/paper cannot be cooked within thirty minutes. It needs to grow. Brood over it during odd moments. Take down points on pieces of papers.
2. Read material / ask GPT only after you have thought yourself dry
3. Collect far more material than you intend to use. 
4. Dont do the rookie mistake of trying to cover all the material that you have gathered.
5. Assemble hundred thoughts and discard ninety. 

### Verbal communication
1. Stress important words and subordinate unimportant ones
2. Change your pitch - voice modulation. Dont make it boring
3. Vary your rate of speaking. Say "Ten crore rupees" quickly and with an air of triviality and it sounds like a very small sum. Say "Ten thousand Rupees"; say it slowly and say it feelingly and it starts feeling like a big amount, as if you are impressed with the hugeness of the amount. Your tone will carry on the message for you and not your words.
4. Pause before and after important ideas - add drama

### Conduct
1. If possible tell your audience to crowd around a small area instead of scattering accross a a big empty hall. 
2. Make sure that the lighting and ventilation is proper - else your audience will go to sleep. Oxygen and light are very important. Keep the air fresh and flood the place with lights
3. Dont hide behind a table/ rostrum.

### Speech writing
1. Arouse curiosity
2. Begin with a story
3. Make the beginning dramatic
4. Use an exibit - something to show to the audience
5. Ask a question.
6. Give shocking facts. 
7. Always relate figures to something tangible
8. Restate your important ideas in different words
9. Use picture building words
